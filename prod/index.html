What to Pack —

Quick Note

Bozeman is located 4,800 ft above sea level.

Weather in Early September

September is typically a comfortable month in Bozeman with high's in the mid 70's and the lows in the mid 40's at night. Rain isn't frequent but make sure to check the forecast in the weeks leading up to your travels.

Last year around this time we had some pretty bad wildfires in the surrounding areas. Please watch the forecast and know that it could be smoky with low visibility. If you're planning on making a trip to Yellowstone National Park or Glacier National Park be aware of possible closures due to inclement weather and or active wildfires.

Your suitcase

Packing to dress in layers would be the wisest decision. Comfortable pants would be helpful if you plan on hiking. A sweatshirt or two to cover yourself at night should keep you warm enough (maybe even light fall jacket). Bring comfortable walking shoes whether you plan to sightsee in town or venture out on a hike!
